About

My name is Marc Leef. I am currently a first year masters student in the computer science department at Princeton University and a recent graduate from the University of Oregon. My research interests include machine learning, network security, and bioinformatics. This summer (2016) I am excited to be joining Pinterest as a Software Engineering Intern. Previously, I've worked at Amazon Web Services on the universal applications team, at Affymetrix within the bioinformatics group, and at Oregon Health & Science University in the Knight Cancer Institute.
